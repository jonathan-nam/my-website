Hello!

My name is Jonathan Nam, and I am a junior at the University of Maryland at College Park, currently pursuing degrees in Computer Science and Linguistics. Some of my interests include software engineering, machine learning, and as you can see here, web development!


This past summer, I worked as a Software Engineering Intern at Alarm.com on the Android Mobile team. As a member of the Android Mobile team, I was able to aid in the development of a home security app reaching over 1,500,000 end users. I learned the fundamentals of mobile development, how to design features, give thorough code reviews, write clean code, and so much more. I am incredibly grateful for this experience, and I'm excited to use what I've learned in the future.


When I'm not coding, I enjoy playing video games, watching the NBA, and exercising (in no particular order - running, weight-lifting, and playing basketball). Thanks for visiting my personal website, created with HTML, CSS, and Javascript.
